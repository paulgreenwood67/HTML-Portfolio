    Digital Art

    Here you can see some of my digital art that I call Urbagraphs.

    Urbagraphs are created using photographs, and items kept from holidays and travels such as tickets and receipts etc. Photographs converted to vectors in Adobe Illustrator and then imported into Abobe Photoshop. Tickets and receipts etc are scanned and also converted to vectors and imported into photoshop. Once all these items are in imported I manipulate the layers in various ways to create the final artworks.

    The original idea behind them was to create artworks from the items people bring back from their travels as a momento of their adventures. Most of the artworks I have created are between 50cm and 200cm in length. However, due to the fact that the artworks are made from vectors they can be scaled up to very large sizes and I have therefore had two commissions to create artwork for wall papers for two Ibis hotels. Photos of these can be seen in the gallery below.
