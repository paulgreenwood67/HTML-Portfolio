    Street Photography

    Street Photography is a passion of mine, but one I have had little opportunity to endulge for the last few years.

    So, what is Street Photography?

    There are a few definitions out there, some I agree with and some I don't. Firstly, although it is called 'Street' photography it does not need to be conducted on a street only, it can be any public space. Some say that photos must include people, this I don't agree with but they should say something about human behaviour. Some say that street photography should depict the 'ordinary' and 'everyday' which has unfortunatly led to the internet being full of extremely 'ordinary' and frankly not very interesting photos.

    For me, the best Street Photography captures the 'extraodinary' in the ordinary. So, here are some of my photos, I hope they live up to my defininition.
