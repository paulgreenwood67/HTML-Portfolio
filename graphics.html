    Graphic Design

    I worked for many years as a Freelance Graphic Designer, mostly for public sector organisations such as Local Authorities like: Preston City Council, Lancahshire County Council and the Greater Manchester Combined Authority. I also worked for community arts organisations like PRESCAP (Preston Community Arts Project) and Spot On, Rural Touring.
