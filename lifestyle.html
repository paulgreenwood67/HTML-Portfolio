    Lifestyle Photography

    Lifestyle Photography or Family Documentary Photography is about creating natural photographs that tell the story of the people in them. The images created are not posed in a pre organised location but rather photographs that capture life as it happens.

    Sometimes there is a little direction to help bring out the best of a situation but this is always minimal and the objective is to create nature, relaxed photographs.
