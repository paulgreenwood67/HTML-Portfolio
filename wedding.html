    Wedding Photography

    My style of Wedding Photography is what is know as the Documentary or Reportage style. That is to say I would try and capture a couples wedding day as it happened with very little intervention or direction from myself. There was always time for a few 'posed' shots but for me I prefered to let they day happen and capture those unique personal moments.

    For me Wedding Photography is (was) something I would do as a sideline for a little extra cash. Covid had a huge effect on the wedding industry including photographers and it has been a few years since I shot a wedding.
